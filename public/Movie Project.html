The Best Movies According to GonçaloDevs

My top 3 movies of all-time are:

The Wolf of Wall Street

Martin Scorsese created a masterpiece and casted some of the best talent Hollywood had, which translated to a oscar-nominated, beautifully orchestrated, motion picture.

Shrek

This is one of my childhood favorites. It is a timeless movie, and will be enjoyed for many years to come, even if just in the GonçaloDevs household!

Birdman

Wonderfully produced movie, where the director Alejandro Gonzalez Inarritu achieved an illusion of a one single shot take.
